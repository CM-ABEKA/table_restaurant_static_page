About Us

PASS DELI É RESTAURANT is a world class restaurant offering you the best of dishes from all over the world.
PASS DELI É RESTAURANT begun as a small fast foods outlet in the busy streets of Nairobi and has grown steadily due to the hard work and dedication of its employees. Beautiful makes a meal tastier is the spirit of the restaurant. For this reason we always choose the best architecture and location for you to enjoy a nice meal with a beautiful scene.

All our 8 branches country wide offer the best services and best prepared dishes.
Welcome and enjoy any time of the day, any day of the week.
